Why Madrid?

Madrid, the sunniest capital in Europe

Madrid boasts an average of 250 cloudless days per year. That's a lot of Vitamin D! It is one of the greenest cities you can visit with over 300,000 trees and 6,400 hectares of green space to enjoy.
Madrid captivates visitors with its vibrant culinary scene. A haven for food enthusiasts, the city boasts its own distinct gastronomy, offering a myriad of delectable options for discerning palates. Traditional tabernas and tapas bars adorn its streets, alongside bustling food markets and fine dining establishments. Here you can even find Restaurante Botin, the oldest restaurant in the world which opened in 1725!
